About Us

Follow us on Instagram!

Welcome to Synchronicity Salon, where beauty is not just a service, but a shared passion passed down through generations. In the heart of Coral Gables, Florida, you’ll find our salon, a labor of love founded by the dynamic mother-daughter duo, Adriana and Paola.

Paola, one of the most sought-after hair professionals in Miami, commenced her meteoric rise in the industry at Lunatic Fringe Boise in 2015. Through her remarkable work ethic and continuous education, she ascended to the rank of Master Stylist in less than five years. A quadruple threat and master of color services, haircuts, hair extensions, and styling, Paola excels in every aspect of her profession, earning her the affectionate title of a “Hair Prodigy” among both peers and clients.

On the other hand, Adriana’s story is one of seasoned expertise and a lifelong dedication to her craft. With roots in Mexico City, she honed her skills at the renowned Robert Lee’s before venturing to the United States. Adriana’s journey led her to prestigious salons like Pueblo Bonito Blanco and Suzanne Morel, where she specialized in bridal hair and makeup.

In 2023, Adriana and Paola found themselves in the same sunny climate of Florida, both working for the best hair studios in the state, and decided it was time to team up. Together, they fuse their rich experiences, creating a salon that reflects their shared values of continuous learning, highly personalized care, and technical mastery.

We invite you to experience the artistry, expertise, and familial warmth that define Synchronicity Salon.

Our Policies

Late Arrival
To ensure all our clients receive the full attention and service they deserve, we kindly request punctuality. If you are more than 20 minutes late for your scheduled appointment, we may be unable to provide the complete service you were booked for. In such cases, a 100% charge of the scheduled service will apply.

Cancelation Policy
We understand that plans change, and unforeseen circumstances arise. Please provide a minimum of 24 hours’ notice for cancellations. Cancellations made within 24 hours of the scheduled appointment will incur a non-refundable charge of 50% of the total hair service.

No Show Policy
In the event that you cannot make your appointment and fail to notify Synchronicity Salon, a 100% charge of the scheduled hair service will be applied.

Hair Service Satisfaction Policy
Your satisfaction is our priority. We offer a one-week window from the original date of your hair service for complimentary adjustments. After one week, any adjustments requested will be the financial responsibility of the client.

Payment Processing Fee
Please be aware that a 3.0% card processing fee is applied. To avoid this fee, we gladly accept payments through Zelle, Venmo, or in cash.

Service Pricing
All services at Synchronicity Salon have a starting price. The final cost may vary based on the length and density of your hair. Our team is happy to provide more detailed pricing information during your consultation.
